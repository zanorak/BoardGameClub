Boardgame Club

The Board Game Club is a place where you can get together with other
people and learn how to play new and interesting board games.
It meets every Tuesday during lunch in Room 21.

Some of the many games you can learn and play are:

5-Minute Dungeon is a chaotic, co-operative, real-time
card game in which players have only five minutes to escape
the randomized dungeon.

Exploding Kittens is a Kitten-Powered Russian Roulette Card Game.
Purr-fect anyone who is into kittens and explosions and laser
beams and sometimes goats.

Learn to play Dungeons and Dragons. The original role playing game.
The one your parents warned you about.
